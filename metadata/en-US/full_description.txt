<p>Kaisoku is a free and open source manga reader for Android.</p>
<p><b>Main Features:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Online manga catalogs (with 1100+ manga sources)</li>
<li>Search manga by name, genres, and more filters</li>
<li>Favorites organized by user-defined categories</li>
<li>Reading history, bookmarks, and incognito mode support</li>
<li>Download manga and read it offline. Third-party CBZ archives are also supported</li>
<li>Clean and convenient Material You UI, optimized for phones, tablets, and desktop</li>
<li>Standard and Webtoon-optimized customizable reader, gesture support on reading interface</li>
<li>Notifications about new chapters with updates feed, manga recommendations (with filters)</li>
<li>Integration with manga tracking services: Shikimori, AniList, MyAnimeList, Kitsu</li>
<li>Password / fingerprint-protected access to the app</li>
<li>Automatically sync app data with other devices on the same account</li>
<li>Support for older devices running Android 5+</li>
</ul>
